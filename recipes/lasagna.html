Eggplant and Goat Cheese Lasagna

Description: this lasagna is a take on the traditional. Instead of noodles, it uses sliced eggplant. Instead of ricotta, it uses goat cheese. It is delicious!!!

  • cooking spray
  • 1 large eggplant, sliced into 1/4 inch rounds
  • 1 (28 ounce) jar garden vegetable pasta sauce
  • 1 (11 ounce) log goat cheese, sliced
  1. Step 1 Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Step 2 Spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
  3. Step 3 Arrange one fourth of eggplant slices in a layer in the bottom of the dish.
  4. Step 4 Pour one fourth of pasta sauce on top of eggplant layer.
  5. Step 5 Dot pasta sauce layer with one fourth of goat cheese slices.
  6. Step 6 Repeat layers three more times with remaining ingredients, ending with a top layer of goat cheese.
  7. Step 7 Bake in preheated oven until pasta sauce is bubbling and goat cheese is melted, 45 to 60 minutes.
